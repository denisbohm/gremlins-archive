Dear Friends and Fellow Collectors,

Dimensional Designs is a company that specializes in Horror, Science Fiction and Fantasy related collectibles. We began our business by producing our own line of latex monster masks, 3D T-shirts, skull paperweights. We are currently manufacturers of our own model figure kits.

Aside from our own studio projects, we do serve as an outlet for items in our specialty from other artists and manufacturers. We hope to bring you some very exciting, high quality pieces of art to add to your own collection.

Recently, we have opened our doors to modelers who have sculpted their own figures and wish to sell limited runs (in kit form). If you are an independent modeler or sculptor we may be interested in carrying your figure and selling your creation through our company. These limited edition fan appreciation model kits are not intended to infringe on the rights of copyright holders. They are solely for the art of collecting, and are sold from collector to collector on a first come first serve basis. They are generally made to be limited edition item.

Always keep in mind that our staff here at Dimensional Designs are collectors as well, we understand the difficulty of finding items to complete or begin a collection. Many new items we purchase are not listed. We may have an item or information on an item you are looking for. So be sure to give us a call or write to us. We are also open for "trades", you may have an item that we are in search of. We trade on listed and non-listed items.

We appreciate your browsing through our web pages, if you wish to reach us by e-mail our address is DimDesigns@aol.com. We hope to hear from you soon. Happy Collecting!
