The Prehistoric Times is a forty page magazine for the dinosaur collector and enthusiast. Each issue has a full color cover and contains reviews of model kits, modeling tips, histories of dino models, toys and sculptures, interviews with people involved in the dinosaur world today and much more. Six issues are published per year. Trial and Back issues are available.
