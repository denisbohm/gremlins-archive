SOME WORDS FROM THE EDITOR

Greetings Kitbuilders! Welcome to issue three, the new and enlarged version. IN THIS ISSUE: A letter column to voice your "yeahs" and "nays", (please be kind, the staff is very sensitive). Also we needed the x-tra pages to FULLY showcase THE WEIRD AND WONDERFUL WORLD OF DAVE BARKER. Here is a guy that is not content with just great building and painting ability, but has to bring his kits alive! We here think you will be as impressed with Daves work as we were. An informative interview with Mike Evans of LUNAR MODELS. With "behind the scenes photos" of "Garage Kit" production. Bill Jenks shares with us a possable solution to the sticky problem of glue removal. For you ALIEN addicts, a sneak preview of info for Aliens 3. Plus the "California connection", "The Morgue", and the ever popular "Collectors Corner". Plus much more from the Old and new days of figure kits.
HAPPY READING!

GORDY!!!
