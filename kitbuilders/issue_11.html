"Groovy" kit builder circa mid 60's

The right to do something does not mean that doing it is right.

A colorful greeting to you, and welcome to #11, our painting tips issue. Bruce Turner of Cinema Art Models explains how to get the most out of a few bottles of paint. Rich larson shares his painting style in a step by step approach on an actual kit. Plus Joe Jobe jumps in and really gives you the facts on when and when not to use different types of paints on vinyl kits.

Also in this issue we share with you a photo review and listing of model contest winners from the Louisville Kentucky Wonderfest "94" show. Mark "The Mad Hatter" Paskell leads us through his rhyming review and photo montage of the Mad Model Party.

All this plus new kit releases and reviews, The "Collectors Corner", classifieds and more.

Enjoy!

Gordy
