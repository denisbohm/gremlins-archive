romoter Paul Antonelli has signed with the Sheraton Tara Castle Hotel in Framingham, Mass. The Sheraton Tara will be the future site of the Monster Modelfest shows, that will be run every August. The old site in Boston was just sold and too many changes are taking place, like price increases on room rates to $150 per night. The Sheraton will provide the show with a great space for the main ballroom, free parking for everyone, room rates starting at $85 per night, minutes outside Boston and a location right off the Mass Tumpike. For Hotel reservations call 508-879-7200 and ask for the Modelfest rate.

"I'm confident that everyone will be very happy with our new location. The Castle is very plush, and very open. We have enough space to have the show in one huge square room, and more than enough space to expand the show. The room will have over 100 8-foot tables and that doesn't include the model contest area. And dealers have already started to book space at the show. We have also started to book our guests for this upcoming show in August. We have confirmed with Bill Hinzman, John Russo, Brinke Stevens and David Fisher so far, along with a few comic book artists and writers, but we're not stopping there." Stated the promoter Paul Antonelli.
