The art of collecting

Garage/resin model figure kits are intended for the "art of collecting" they are not created to infringe upon copyrights, only to supply a small amount of collectors with rare pieces of art done by the various artists available to us today.

By the way...

All model figure kits are available on a limited supply. Sold from one collector to another, on a first come first sold basis. Some items will not be available once they have sold out.

Buy-Bye

Once an item is deleted, there is very little chance we will bring it back. We hope to bring you a new variety of original kits each time we put out a new list. Be sure to order those kits you are interested in now. We can not guarantee that we will have them the next time around.

Return policy

If for any reason you are not happy with your purchase, you may return it within 10 working days for a refund, credit, replacement or exchange. (20 working days for overseas orders). Merchandise must be in the original condition as received. Any shipping charges incurred will be deducted from the amount refunded and there will be a 10% restocking fee.

For orders outside of the USA

The shipping costs on orders shipped by air outside of the USA total about 35% of total order, this depends on the weight and dimensions of the package.

Discounts

If you place an order for merchandise of $250.00 or more, your are entitled to a 10% discount on your order (not to include shipping charges).

The 10% discount applies only to model kit orders. (it does not include such specialty items such as the Crest of Dracula ring).

If you have any discount coupons, be sure to include them with your order. Remember.... some coupons cannot be used with others. Please read them carefully.
