ant to add something to the gallery? Share a kit review? Or announce a new kit release from your company? You've come to the right place! Best of all - it's all completely free!

Before you can add any information you must first send e-mail to gremlins@gremlins.com and request a logon account. Please provide your first and last name, the password that you would like to use, and let us know what types of additions you would like to make (gallery entries, kit reviews, and/or bites press releases).

Once you receive your account information you can Logon. After logging on you will see some extra buttons show up when you view buildups, kits, contacts, etc. These buttons allow you to add information to the web site by filling out various forms.
