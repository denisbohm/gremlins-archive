Location

Meadowlands Hilton Hotel
2 Harmon Plaza
Secaucus, New Jersey

Call (201) 348-6900 for reservations and make sure you ask for the special "Chiller Theatre" rate. Reservations MUST be made by April 1st, 1997.

The Meadowlands Hilton is located less than 1/4 of a mile from the New Jersey Turnpike Exit 16W. It is located only 2 miles from the Lincoln Tunnel.

From the N.J. Turnpike North or South:
Take Exit 16 West to Route 3 East to the Meadowlands Parkway Exit. At the bottom of the ramp turn left onto the Meadowlands Parkway. At first light make a right onto Harmon Plaza. The Meadowlands Hilton is located at the end of the road on the left.

Easy access from Manhattan:
Take the Lincoln Tunnel lo Route 3 West to the Meadowlands Parkway Exit. At bottom of ramp turn left onto the Meadowlands Parkway. At 2nd stoplight turn right on to Harmon Plaza. Hotel is at the end on the left.

Spring Extravaganza

This is the show that New Jersey film fanatics have been waiting for. We have gathered together under one roof, some of the greatest stars and legends of the science fiction, fantasy, and horror genre plus the top movie memorabilia, toy, collectibles, video, and model kit dealers from the entire United States and as far away as Japan.

Model Customizing Contest

Model kit builders and dealers from all over the country will be competing in this model customizing contest. Kits will feature creatures from the past and the present, from some of our favorite sci-fi, fantasy and horror movies. Last year some of the most incredible kits were entered. Be sure not to miss this event. Contest will be held on Saturday only at the 2nd floor exhibit area. Registration is from 11am to 2pm Saturday only - no exceptions. Model contest rules.

The Largest Horror / Sci-Fi Dealers Room On The East Coast

Our 2 large dealers rooms will be loaded with some of the most fantastic movie memorabilia, posters, lobby cards, stills, toys, magazines, model kits, videos and books ever assembled for your buying pleasure.
