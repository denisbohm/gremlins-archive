Screamin' Products is pleased to announce a new line of original female figure kits - the "SCREAMIN' QUEENS". The first of these limited-edition collectibles is "Cylena, Queen of the Night", and will be available in January-February 1997.

The Queen of the Night is a one-seventh scale resin model kit requiring limited assembly. The lovely Cylena is guaranteed to quicken the pulse of any red-blooded collector; she is crowned with a flying mane of raven hair and clothed in a wind-blown cloak (and not much else!)

Each Screamin' Queen will come with a booklet which describes the legend of the Screamin' Queens and the mythical history of each particular Queen. In months to come, look for the Queen of Outer Space, the Queen of the Dead, the Queen of Swords and many more.

Screamin' Products is pleased to announce a new upcoming model figure kit: Luke Skywalker from the Star Wars film trilogy. This exciting new model is officially licensed through LucasArts Entertainment. The Official Luke Skywalker model kit is the twelfth release in Screamin's popular Science Fiction series, and the ninth 1/4 scale Star Wars figure. Screamin's Luke kit is an exact replica of the famous movie character, dressed in black as seen in Return of the Jedi. Luke is posed brandishing his light saber, and is designed to duel with Screamin's 1/4 scale Darth Vader kit. When assembled, this awesome vinyl figure will stand approximately 17" tall. Sculpted by Jeff Brower, the Luke Skywalker model kit will make an impressive addition to the Screaming Star Wars line. The suggested retail price is $67.95 and the kit is available now.
