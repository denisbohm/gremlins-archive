Farmingham Sheraton Tara Directions

Directions: The Farmingham Sheraton Tara is located just off the Massachusetts Turnpike at Exit 12, Route 9 West. It's only 25 miles west of Boston, and is ideally located next to high tech and industrial complexes on Route 128 and 495. Regularly scheduled limousine service to Boston's Logan Airport. Ample free parking.
