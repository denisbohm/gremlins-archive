SOME WORDS FROM THE EDITOR

GREETINGS! Kit builders, and welcome to issue #4 of the magazine. In this issue we showcase the fantasy like world of Mr. Bob Burns. His Collection of original, old and new, movie props is hard to believe. Some of the pieces he has have already been made into kits, with the possiblity of others on the horizon.

We also would like to announce two new feature's in this issue. "Tales From The Script" which deals with future anld current film projects with garage kit potential. Also "Kit Spotlight" a regular featute that spotlights a single kit and describes it in more detail.

Terry Webb is back to share with you some of his favorite busts! (Sorry, we don't mean Monique Gabrielle, or Brinke Stevens.) Those "Limbless" creations that do not get the exposure they deserve.

Least we not forget "Cool New Stuff", "Collectors Corner", "The Morgue", and what ever else we can sqeeze in! ENJOY!!!!

GORDY!!!
