Junior Model Expo: FREE show and contest for finished model kits built by con members up to age 16.

*Prizes


Polar Lights Model Kits: Robbie the Robot from Forbidden Planet, Curly from the 3 Stooges


Colpar Hobbies West : Babylon 5 Space Station Kit

*Open Judging Sytem:
Each model entered is evaluated for its own merits by our team of experienced judges.
Each modeler will get the score cards used by the judges.
Being judged with this system can help improve your skills.

*Schedule:

Friday: 6 to10 PM Registration and Viewing.
Saturday: 9 to 12 PM Registration and Viewing
  12 to 4 PM Viewing
  4:30 PM Awards and Prizes
  5 PM JME closes.

MIA is an unaffiliated model club that meets several times each month at Hobby Town-Citadel crossing in Colorado Spring, CO. Meetings start at 6PM and are usually held on the 2nd and 4th Sundays of each month.

Our Mission:

      Promoting fun and learning in modelling

Our Membership:

      Varied ages, interests and skill levels

Our Activities:

      Meeting, Make-N-Takes, Demos and Contests
