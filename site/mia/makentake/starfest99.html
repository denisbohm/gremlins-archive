StarFest 99 Make 'N Take


On April 17 & 18, we held another make-n-take at the Star Fest science fiction convention. Over 40 new modellers got to try their hand at making and painting World War II aircraft. Between the Make-N-Take and the Model Show, the MIA volunteers were kept very busy, but a great time was still had by all!
