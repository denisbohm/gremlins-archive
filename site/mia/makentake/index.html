New info on next Make 'N Take:

WOW! If you haven't had a chance to participate in a Make N' Take before, this is the one to attend! AMT/ERTL has graciously donated Star Wars Episode One kits for this make 'n take and they're going to go fast! In addition to being able to build your model using supplies provided by Testors and Excel Corporations and assistance from MiA volunteers, you also get to paint your model kit with airbrushes provided by Aztek. This event will be open to first time modelers between the ages of 7 and 16 and costs nothing to participate! Thanks to our sponsors, you get to build, paint, and take home your kit absolutely free! Thank you to all of the sponsors and volunteers that make this possible!


In addition to the make n' take event, modelers over the age of 16 can also participate if they would like. For a $10 fee, you will receive a Geometric Riker vinyl figure kit (a $49.99 retail value) and the use of our tools and assistance in building and painting the kit.
