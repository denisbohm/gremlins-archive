StarCon 98 Make 'N Take


Our second event was held at Star Con in August 1998 at the DIA Holiday Inn. This is one of two large scale scifi cons held yearly in Denver and sponsored by Starland. A nice review of this event can be seen at the Starland web page. This event included an Airbrush Test Drive with Aztek airbrushes and supplies provided by the Testors Corp. Tom Grossman's status as an industry professional made this possible.
