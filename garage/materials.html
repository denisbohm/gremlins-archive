Preparing Materials

If you want to prepare and send your materials electronically, here are a couple of guidelines:
  • Design your images for display at 72 dpi.
  • Try to keep image width below 8 inches.
  • Provide large images in JPEG/JFIF format.
  • Try to keep large image file sizes below 50KB.
  • Provide small icon images in GIF format (256 color, interlaced).
  • Gallery icons are interlaced GIF format, with dimensions of 64 by 96 pixels.
  • For text use simple text files.

Sending Materials

You can ftp files to ftp.gremlins.com and send mail to gremlins@gremlins.com to let me know that you have transfered your files.

Or bundle up your files using Zip or StuffIt archives and send them as attachments to gremlins@gremlins.com.
