Action Kits International
Barnabas Collins Bust
Amazing Figure Modeler Review

Double Vision review by David Fisher and Terry Webb from Amazing Figure Modeler Issue 3.

Action Kits International have been producing sophisticated model kits based on Dark Shadows, both the newer version as well as the classic daytime series. The latest entry is a striking study of Jonathan Frid as the tragic vampire Barnabas Collins. If you are a fan of the TV show as I am, this bust is a must. I like the fact that it is a serious portrait study more than the snarling, open mouthed vampire you might expect. Sculptor bill Merklein has done a beautiful job with the details and features, yet when I study the likeness, I feel that there is something slightly off in the proportions. At the same time, when you break down the features, they all look correct, so I can't exactly pinpoint what I feel the culprit might be. I'm probably being to knit-picky since I'm a big fan of the character! The castings are beautiful- I had to hunt for the seam line and for the price, you can't beat it. A classy piece, I recommend it to the fans. DF (David Fisher)

Now here is a piece that should silence every critic of resin casted sculptures. Don't believe me? Then check out these facts. It's beautifully sculpted and its cast requires no assembly or clean up work whatsoever, and it's inexpensive. In fact this is such a nice production that you don't even have to print it for it to look good - just set it on the shelf. Kudos to Action Kits International and Bill Merklein for taking the time and effort to give resin not just a good name, but a great one! TW (Terry Webb)
