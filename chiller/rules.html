The Finest Science Fiction, Fantasy, Horror, Film & TV Memorabilia Show In The U.S.A.

Presented by Chiller Theatre Magazine in association with Something Weird Video

Contest registration is from Saturday 11am to 2pm only. All entries must be recieved by this time - no exceptions.

All Chiller show staff are ineligible to enter the model and art contest.

Any model entered must be built and painted by the modeler entering the contest.

If you produce, offer a build up service in hobby related magazines, or have a table at the Chiller Show offering same, you must enter in the professional, diorama, or scratch built catagories only.

Once your kit is officially entered, please do not touch it. Ask a staffer for assistance.

Kits exceeding a size limit of 18"x13&quotx18" are subject to an additional entry fee.

No business cards are permitted on contest tables.

Do not touch another entrants kit.

Any violation of the rules set forth can and will result in a disqualification without a refund of your admission fee.

Any and all protests must be made in writing to a contest official within 1 hour of the awards presentation.

All desisions made by the Judges and staff are final.

Starting with issue #7 all contest winners and their kits will be posted in the Chiller Theatre Magazine Resin Report.

Chiller Theatre, their staff, and contest organizers are not responsible for any kit list, stolen, or damaged during th course of the contest.
